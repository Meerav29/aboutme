Career Timeline

2024 – AI Solutions for Advising

  • Developed an academic advising chatbot using commercial LLMs and APIs for the College of IST to assist students with course selections and academic queries, reducing student advising load by ~35%.
  • Published a research paper for ACM SIGCSE (2025) on the development, implementation, and impact of the chatbot, exploring its influence on students and relevance in the current academic landscape.

2024 – UAV Research on Icing Clouds

  • Conducted research on the impact of cloud and icing conditions on UAV performance using torque & RPM data: +/- 25%.
  • Developed methodology to monitor propeller performance using onboard computers and performed data analysis.
  • Proposed real-time algorithms to mitigate icing effects and enhance drone reliability.

2024 – Research Award + AI Advising Chatbot

  • Continued REU research in Spring 2024 and was awarded Emerging Undergraduate Researcher of the Year.
  • Got accepted into the MC REU Summer Program to work on Autonomous UAV research in the Vertical Lift Research Center of Excellence.
  • Pitched an idea to streamline academic advising using AI-powered chatbots at the College of IST and got accepted to work on this project.

2023 – HTI Lab, Penn State

  • Assisted in driving simulator experiments evaluating driver behavior amid varying autonomous vehicle presence (0–100%).
  • Built simulation environments and wrote documentation using proprietary STISIM3 software for four road scenarios.

2022 – Joined Tide Foundation

  • I joined the Tide Foundation – a non-profit that helps underprivileged kids get a cohesive and well-rounded education. I shadowed the founder Dr. Jwalin Patel and worked on logistics, marketing, operations, course design and – of course – teaching underprivileged kids.

2020 – Founded Lirem

  • I started a project called Lirem with the intention of helping kids in my neighborhood become good readers. Very soon the interest was city-wide, revenue was climbing and our model was attracting more parents and student volunteers. However due to COVID and extensive lockdowns in India, we could not keep it going.
  • Great learning experience - taught me a lot on what not to do as a founder, but most importantly, I learned ownership and quick decision making, which I value the most in my work today.

2019 – Internship at Drone Manufacturer

  • Interned at Electrobotics in Ahmedabad, India after robotics championship.
  • Shadowed development team, observing design and testing of various drone technologies.

2019 – National Robotics Finalist

  • Led a team of 3 students and 2 advisors in designing an AWD RC car with a 4-axis robotic arm using acrylic components.
  • Qualified for national finals of a robotics championship in India for innovation and performance.
